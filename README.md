# Maroon5CarRentals
Maroon5 Car Rentals is an Application that allows a Customer to book a Taxi or an Auto for a ride/rental to reach their required, preferred destination at their convenience . The Application also allows Customers to view their Ride History and convey any inconvenience that occurred during/after the ride to the admin. Even Drivers can sign up and get rides allotted to them on a random basis. The Application allows the Drivers to view their Booking History. The Admin controls the ride fare ,the destinations that can be booked , view and remove the drivers(if necessary), view the complete booking history and the complaints/ feedback given by both the customer and the driver.
